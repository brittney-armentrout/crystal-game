Crystal Collector Conundrum

Collect if you dare!

RULES: You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
       There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your player score to the target score. You lose the game if your score exceeds the target score.
       The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
